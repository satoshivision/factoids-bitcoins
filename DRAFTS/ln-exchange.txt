LN allows to use exchanges in safe way,
that is
the exchange can not
run away
or "get hacked"
or steal
your funds that you prepared to give them via LN.

Therefore you can at cost of ~1 bitcoin tx
open LN with say 0.1 BTC
and then send to exchange even 0.00001 BTC one-by-one
and getting paid (in altcoin or fiat).
